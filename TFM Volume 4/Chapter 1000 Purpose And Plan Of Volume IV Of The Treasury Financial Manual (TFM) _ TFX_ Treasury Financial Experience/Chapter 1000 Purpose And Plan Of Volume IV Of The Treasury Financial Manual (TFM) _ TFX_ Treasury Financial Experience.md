# Chapter 1000 Purpose And Plan Of Volume IV Of The Treasury Financial Manual (TFM)

## <span id="page-0-0"></span>**Purpose And Plan Of Volume IV Of The Treasury Financial Manual (TFM)**

#### **[Introduction](#page-0-0)**

Volume IV provides procedural instructions to other concerned parties aside from federal departments and agencies, Federal Reserve Banks acting as depositaries and fiscal agents of the United States, and financial institutions acting as depositaries and financial agents of the government, consistent with authority in the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR), or other authority.

#### <span id="page-0-1"></span>**Section [1010—Scope](#page-0-1) and Applicability**

The TFM is the Department of the Treasury's (Treasury's) official publication of policies, procedures, and instructions concerning financial management in the federal government. It is intended to promote the government's financial integrity and operational efficiency.

<span id="page-0-2"></span>See the **[About](https://tfx.treasury.gov/tfm/about-tfm) TFM** section for more information.

#### **Section [1015—Authority](#page-0-2)**

<span id="page-0-3"></span>See, *inter alia*, 31 U.S.C. 301, 306, 321, 323; 31 U.S.C. Chapters 31, 33, 35, 37; 31 CFR; 31 CFR Part 203.

#### **Section [1020—Composition](#page-0-3) of Volume IV**

Volume IV contains only one part as follows:

Part 1: Introduction—Describes the Purpose and Plan of Volume IV, provides guidance to financial institutions that process federal tax collections using the Electronic Federal Tax Payment System and the Federal Tax Application, and references the TFM chapter that gives instructions to financial institutions that participate in the Treasury Investment Program.

<span id="page-1-0"></span>**Section [1025—Types](#page-1-0) of TFM Releases**

The TFM is comprised of the following types of releases:

- Chapters,
- Bulletins, and
- Supplements.

<span id="page-1-1"></span>See the **[About](https://tfx.treasury.gov/tfm/about-tfm) TFM** section on our website for more information about these types of releases.

**Section [1030—Subscribing](#page-1-1) to the TFM Notification for TFM Updates**

Users may subscribe to the **email [notification](mailto:join-tfm-vol-1@lists.fiscal.treasury.gov) service** for TFM updates. The email notifies users of a TFM update and provides a link to the updated TFM release.
