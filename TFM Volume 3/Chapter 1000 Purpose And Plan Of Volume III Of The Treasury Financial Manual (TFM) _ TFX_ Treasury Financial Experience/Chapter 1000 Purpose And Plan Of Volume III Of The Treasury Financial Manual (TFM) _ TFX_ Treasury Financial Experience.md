# Chapter 1000 Purpose And Plan Of Volume III Of The Treasury Financial Manual (TFM)

## <span id="page-0-0"></span>**Purpose And Plan Of Volume III Of The Treasury Financial Manual (TFM)**

#### **[Introduction](#page-0-0)**

<span id="page-0-1"></span>Volume III guides financial institutions designated by Treasury to act as depositaries and financial agents of the government.

#### **Section 1010 Scope and [Applicability](#page-0-1)**

Volume III guides financial institutions designated by Treasury to act as depositaries and financial agents of the government.

#### **Section 1010—Scope and Applicability**

The TFM is the Department of the Treasury's (Treasury's) official publication of policies, procedures, and instructions concerning financial management in the federal government. It is intended to promote the government's financial integrity and operational efficiency.

<span id="page-0-2"></span>See the **[About](https://tfx.treasury.gov/tfm/about-tfm) TFM** section for more information.

#### **Section [1015—Authority](#page-0-2)**

<span id="page-0-3"></span>See, inter alia, 12 U.S.C. 90, 265, 266; 31 U.S.C. 301, 306, 321, 323; 31 U.S.C. Chapters 31, 33, 35, and 37.

#### **Section [1020—Composition](#page-0-3) of Volume III**

Volume III is divided into the following parts:

- Part 1: Introduction—Describes the purpose and plan of Volume III.
- Part 2: Domestic Depositaries and Financial Agents—Provides guidance that is specific to domestic depositaries and financial agents.

- Part 3: Depositaries and Financial Agents in Foreign Countries, U.S. Territories, or Insular Possessions—Provides guidance that is specific to depositaries and financial agents outside the boundaries of the United States.
- <span id="page-1-0"></span>Part 5: Other Fiscal Matters—Provides guidance on depositary requirements for securing federal agency funds on deposit.

**Section [1025—Types](#page-1-0) of TFM Releases**

The TFM is comprised of the following types of releases:

- Chapters,
- Bulletins, and
- Supplements.

<span id="page-1-1"></span>See the **[About](https://tfx.treasury.gov/tfm/about-tfm) TFM** section on our website for more information about these types of releases.

**Section [1030—Subscribing](#page-1-1) to the TFM Notification for TFM Updates**

Users may subscribe to the **email [notification](mailto:join-tfm-vol-1@lists.fiscal.treasury.gov) service** for TFM updates. The email notifies users of a TFM update and provides a link to the updated TFM release.
