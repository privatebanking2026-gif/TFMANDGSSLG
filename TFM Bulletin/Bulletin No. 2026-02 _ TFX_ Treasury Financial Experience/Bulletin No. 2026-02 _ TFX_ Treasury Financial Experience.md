# Bulletin No. 2026-02

**To:** Heads of Government Departments, Agencies, and Others Concerned

**Subject:** U.S. Standard General Ledger (USSGL) — A Treasury Financial Manual (TFM) Supplement

**1. Purpose -** This bulletin revises the **[USSGL](https://tfx.treasury.gov/tfm/supplements/ussgl)** and replaces all previous amendments.

#### **2. Rescission**

Bulletin No. 2026-02 rescinds the following publications:

- Bulletin No. 2025-02 (December 2024)
- Bulletin No. 2025-04 (March 2025)
- Bulletin No. 2025-06 (June 2025)
- Bulletin No. 2025-08 (September 2025)

#### **3. Explanation of Updates**

Bulletin No. 2026-02 USSGL includes two parts:

- Part 1 FY 2026, replaces USSGL Part 1 FY 2025 reporting.
- Part 2 FY 2027, replaces USSGL Part 2 FY 2026 reporting.

#### **4. Overall Changes to the USSGL**

- Part 1 FY 2026—Revised
- Part 2 FY 2027—Revised
- Summary of Changes—The Summary of Changes identifies specific changes made to the USSGL.
